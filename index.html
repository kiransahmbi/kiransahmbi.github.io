Dashboard Monitoring Air Quality

Canada has over 22 sites across the country monitoring levels of various atmosphere gases such as CO2, CH4, N2, etc.

In order to prevent data loss, I created a visual dashboard using python, pandas, and matplotlib.

Two million daily data points are monitored to remain within upper and lower bounds. If not, an alert email is triggered.
